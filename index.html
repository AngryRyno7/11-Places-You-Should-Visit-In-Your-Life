Rome, Italy

Rome is known for the Colosseum. The Colosseum was first used for Gladiator fights and wild animal fights. Gladiators were usually slaves, criminals, or war prisoners. The Gladiators fought for their freedom but unfortunately someone always dies in each fight. Criminals sentenced to death were sometimes put into the arena without any weapons or armor. The Colosseum is now used as an amphitheater. An amphitheater is an open air venue used for entertainment, performances and sports. Rome was founded on April 21, 752 BC




Orlando, Florida

Orlando, Florida is home to the one and only Disney World. Disney World is a very popular vacation place that a lot of kids dream of going to. Disney World opened in 1971 in Orlando,Florida. Disney World attracted 28.4 million visitors by 1990. The entire Disney World resort is around 40 square miles. Orlando, Florida was founded some time in 1875.




Washington, D.C

In 1790 the federal government established a 100 sq. mile area that was the official home of the white house. This area is called the District of Columbia. There is many things to do in D.C, you can drive by the White House, go to a café, visit a number of museums, visit the national mall, or visit the Washington monument. Washington D.C is not an official state of the U.S, but it is the capital of the United States.




Nashville, Tennessee

Nashville, Tennessee was founded in 1806. Nashville is the 23rd most popular city in the U.S. Some things you can do in Nashville are, going to a Broadway show, visit the Johnny Cash Museum, visit the Country Hall of Fame Museum, go to the Walk of Fame Park, and finally, visit the Frist Art Museum. Nashville’s Centennial Park is home to a complete replica of the Greek parthenon.




Kenya, Afica

Kenya was founded December 12, 1963. Kenya is home to a variety of unique animals like Grevy’s Zebra, The Black Rhino, African Wild Dog. One traditional dance is Kidifu it is one of the Kenyan cultural dances that is performed at a funeral and one of the most commonly performed traditional dances. Some things you could do there are you could go on a Wildlife Safari, River Raft the Tana River, and finally, Climb Mount Kenya.




Paris, France

Paris is known for the Eiffel Tower. The Eiffel Tower is a very famous and beautiful landmark made for the 1889 World's Fair. The World’s Fair was a big gathering of the world. The World’s Fair was kind of like The Olympics But instead it was for a lot of new inventions and new products. The exact date of Paris’s Founding Is unknown but it is certain that it was in the 3rd Century.




Tasmania, Australia

Tasmania was founded in 1642. Tasmania is the world’s 26th largest island. Tasmania has the world’s cleanest air. Some holidays they celebrate are Boxing Day, Queen’s Birthday, Australia Day. Some things you can do in Tasmania are visit Wellington Park, Three Capes Track, Bonorong Wildlife Sanctuary, and lastly, Freycinet National Park.




San Francisco

San Francisco is home to the very famous Golden Gate Bridge. The Golden Gate bridge is possibly the most beautiful bridge in the world. The designer of the Golden Gate Bridge was Joseph Baermann Strauss. The Golden Gate Bridge is 746 feet above sea level at its highest peak. It is also 8981 feet across. San francisco was founded on June 29, 1776.




Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona was founded November 29, 1899. Barcelona is well known for their amazing architecture. They are also known for their soccer team, FC Barcelona. The most popular player on FC Barcelona is Lionel Messi. Some things you could do there are go to a city beach, visit the Barcelona City History Museum, watch FC Barcelona’ play at Camp Nou, and finally, visit the Park Güell.




New York City, New York

New York is known for the Statue of Liberty. The Statue of Liberty was a gift of friendship from France to United States. The Statue of Liberty is 305 feet tall. The Statue of Liberty weighs 450,000 pounds. New York was founded some time in 1624 but the exact date is unknown.




St. Louis, Missouri

St Louis is known for the Gateway Arch. The Gateway Arch (also known as the St. Louis Arch) is a monument built for St. Louis. It is the largest arch in the world. It stands 630 feet tall and weighs 900 tons. The St. Louis Arche’s construction started on February 12, 1963 and ended on October 28, 1965. St Louis was founded some time in 1764.
